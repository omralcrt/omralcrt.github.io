Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 21, 2026  ·  Controller: Ömral Cörüt

This Privacy Policy describes how FlickPad ("the App", "we", "our") handles information when you use the FlickPad iOS application. We respect your privacy and collect only what is strictly necessary to operate the App.

1. What FlickPad Does

FlickPad turns your iPhone or iPad into a wireless trackpad for a nearby Mac on the same local network. All trackpad input (pointer movement, clicks, scrolling, gestures) is sent directly from your device to your Mac over an encrypted connection on your local Wi-Fi network. This data never leaves your network and is never stored on any server.

2. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The App stores the following data only on your device, using iOS system storage (UserDefaults and the Keychain):

  • App settings — pointer speed, natural scroll preference, haptic feedback settings, and gesture toggles.
  • Pairing data — the name and a cryptographic certificate fingerprint of each Mac you have paired, so the App can reconnect securely without repeating the pairing process.
  • Device identifier — a randomly generated ID used to identify your device to paired Macs. It is not linked to your Apple ID or any personal account.
  • Last connected Mac — the name of the last Mac you connected to, used for automatic reconnection.

All of this data remains on your device. It is deleted when you uninstall the App or use the "Reset pairing" option in Settings.

3. Local Network Communication

The App uses your local Wi-Fi network to discover and connect to your Mac. All communication is encrypted with TLS (Transport Layer Security). No trackpad data is routed through the internet or any external server. The App will prompt you for local network permission; without it, the App cannot discover or connect to your Mac.

4. In-App Purchases — RevenueCat

FlickPad offers an optional Pro subscription. Purchases are processed through Apple's App Store. We use RevenueCat (RevenueCat, Inc., San Francisco, CA) to manage subscription status. When you make or restore a purchase, RevenueCat receives:

  • A purchase receipt from Apple (does not contain your name or payment details)
  • Your device type and iOS version
  • Your country (as reported by Apple)
  • A randomly generated user identifier

RevenueCat does not receive your name, email address, or any trackpad usage data. For details, see RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

We do not use advertising networks, behavioral analytics SDKs, or any tracking identifiers (IDFA). The App does not contain ads.

5. Data We Do Not Collect

  • Your name, email address, or Apple ID
  • Your location
  • Camera, microphone, or contacts
  • Trackpad gestures or usage patterns
  • Crash reports sent to external servers
  • Any analytics or behavioral data

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal data from children.

7. Your Rights

Under applicable law, including Turkey's Personal Data Protection Law (KVKK, Law No. 6698), you have the right to:

  • Learn whether your personal data is being processed
  • Request information about the purpose and manner of processing
  • Request correction or deletion of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Object to processing that results in adverse consequences

Because FlickPad does not collect personal data itself, most of these rights apply to data held by Apple (via the App Store) or RevenueCat. You can delete all locally stored App data by using "Reset pairing" in Settings and then uninstalling the App.

For any privacy-related requests, contact us at omralcrt@gmail.com.

8. Data Security

All communication between the App and your Mac is encrypted with TLS. Pairing credentials are stored in the iOS Keychain. We do not transmit your data over the internet, which is the most effective protection against remote interception.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
